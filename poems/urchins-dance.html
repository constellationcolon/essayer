The Urchins' Dance
John M. Lyly

By the moon we sport and play, With the night begins our day; As we frisk the dew doth fall; Trip it, little urchins all! Lightly as the little bee, Two by two and three by three; And about go we, and about go we!
Noyes, A. (Ed.). (19--). In The Magic Casement: An Anthology of Fairy Poetry. London: Chapman & Hall, Ltd. https://archive.org/details/magiccasementant00noyerich
